About NearNest

Connecting Pune's Local Businesses with the Community

Get Connected With Them, Who See You As God!

Our Mission 🚀

NearNest is dedicated to helping residents discover local businesses around them. Whether you're searching for the best cafes, reliable electricians, or hidden gems in your neighborhood, we've got you covered.

Why Use Our Platform? 🌐

  • ✅ Easily explore and find nearby businesses
  • ✅ Search by categories or locations
  • ✅ Add your own business for free
  • ✅ Promote local growth and visibility

Who Can Join? 🧾

Any business operating in Pune — from small street vendors to well-known outlets — can register and showcase their offerings to a wider audience.

Made by Students 👨‍💻

This project is built by passionate students with a mission to digitize Pune’s local marketplace.
